Just shipped a major update to **Depths of Ruin**, my roguelike dungeon crawler built with Godot 4.x!

## New Features

### Key and Door System
- Keys now spawn on each floor
- Locked doors block the stairs until you find a key
- Adds strategic exploration decisions

### New Enemy: Djinn
- Created from smokeless fire (Quran 55:15)
- Ethereal wavy form with malevolent glow
- Spawns on higher floors

### New Equipment
- **Ring of Vampirism** — 5% lifesteal on attacks
- **Amulet of Fury** — +2 ATK, +10% crit chance
- **Greatsword** — +4 ATK, -1 DEF
- **Spear** — +2 ATK, +1 DEF

### Floor Themes
The dungeon now has 5 visual themes that cycle every 5 floors:
- Dungeon (classic stone)
- Cave (earthy browns)
- Crypt (purple-tinted)
- Volcanic (red/orange glow)
- Ice (blue crystals)

### Trap System
- Spike traps — physical damage
- Fire vents — burn damage over time
- Poison darts — poison status
- Teleport traps — random relocation
- Ice patches — slow effect
- Lava cracks — heavy burn damage
- Shadow pits — damage + teleport
- Spirit wisps — XP drain

### Inventory UI
- Press `I` to open inventory
- View equipped weapon, armor, and accessory
- Unequip items with SPACE
- See stat bonuses on each piece

### Shop System
- Press `S` on any floor to visit the shop
- Buy potions, scrolls, and permanent upgrades
- Uses gold collected during runs

Built with Godot 4.x, deployed to GitHub Pages. Mobile-friendly with touch controls and D-pad.
